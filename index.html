Our Activities

At Cebu Technological University - Danao Campus, we believe in the importance of a well-rounded education that extends beyond the classroom. Our activities are designed to foster personal and professional growth, community engagement, and teamwork. Here are some of the key activities we organize:

  • Workshops and Seminars: Regular workshops and seminars on various topics to enhance skills and knowledge.
  • Community Outreach: Initiatives aimed at giving back to the community and promoting social responsibility.
  • Cultural Events: Celebrating diversity through cultural showcases and events that highlight local traditions.
  • Sports Competitions: Encouraging teamwork and physical fitness through inter-school competitions.
  • Research Conferences: Providing a platform for students to present their research and innovations.

Join us as we engage in meaningful activities that enrich our university experience!

About the CTU - Danao Campus

Cebu Technological University – Danao Campus is a premier institution of higher learning located in the heart of Danao City, Cebu. Known for its commitment to academic excellence and innovative research, the campus offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs that cater to the diverse needs of students. With state-of-the-art facilities, a dynamic learning environment, and a highly qualified faculty, CTU Danao equips students with the knowledge and skills to excel in their chosen fields. The campus is deeply engaged with the local community, fostering partnerships that contribute to regional development and global competitiveness. Whether you are pursuing a career in technology, business, education, or other disciplines, CTU Danao provides a nurturing and forward-thinking environment for your academic and professional growth.
